Burlesque
=========

Burlesque is a concatenative, dynamic typed, lazy, probably esoteric programming language.

```
blsq ) 1234 100\/0 9rn.+10coSPsh
9 7 3 8 3 4 3 1 3 7
4 2 5 2 8 7 3 3 4 2
2 3 0 6 4 1 9 7 0 2
5 4 2 1 0 9 1 7 8 8
1 3 0 6 8 2 9 5 7 2
4 8 3 5 8 1 4 5 1 6
2 4 1 8 4 8 2 3 0 4
1 2 7 1 5 8 8 5 5 8
8 2 7 3 8 1 1 2 6 0
8 4 1 7 1 6 7 3 6 8
```
